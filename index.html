About Me

I'm a 15-year-old fullstack developer. I was coding on Scratch for a long time before I switched to "actual" programming languages when I was 11. I coded on Replit for 2 years before I quit due to changes. I now primarily code on VS Code, often using Github to publish my projects. I am a member of Hack Club, which is an online community of teens that code together. I've run a couple of events for Hack Club, like Hacklet and Twist. Combined they received over 150 submissions and we gave out thousands of dollars in prizes. I also helped with Hack Club's Summer of Making, and I'm a member of their team. Recently, I attended Daydream, a worldwide hackathon by Hack Club for teenagers! Right now, I'm working on some new events for Hack Club and coding fun side projects along the way.
